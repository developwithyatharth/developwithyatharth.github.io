Car Care Tips

✔ Wash your vehicle regularly to protect the paint from dust and pollution.

✔ Check tyre pressure before long journeys for better fuel efficiency and safety.

✔ Keep an emergency toolkit, tyre inflator and first-aid kit inside your vehicle.

✔ Replace worn-out wiper blades and maintain proper visibility during rainy seasons.

✔ Use quality interior cleaners and microfiber cloths to maintain your dashboard and seats.
